Backpack

Here’s what I will be carrying along :

  • A short, a jean and their devil child, a hybrid short/jean.
  • Three T-shirt
  • Five underpants
  • Five socks
  • A coat and a sweater
  • Walking shoe and street shoe
  • Sunglasses
  • Swimsuit
  • Towel
  • Silk bag (To sleep)
  • Gourd
  • Toilet kit
  • Secret pocket, to put money and ID
  • Sketch book
  • Knife
  • Small laptop

Adding the bag, we’re reaching 10 kg, which is fine ! The bag can contain 50L, so there’s some place left even when all the gears are in it.
I could have bought a smaller bag, and take less or lighter stuff, but it would have asked too much thinking process.
